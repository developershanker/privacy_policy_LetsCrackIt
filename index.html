Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to LetsCrackIt ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account data

  • Name and display name (provided by Google Sign-In)
  • Email address (provided by Google Sign-In)
  • Profile photo URL (provided by Google Sign-In)

Health & fitness data you enter manually

  • Body weight and height entries you record in the app
  • BMI calculation history with timestamps
  • Date of birth and biological sex (used for age-accurate BMI calculation)

Health data read from Google Health Connect (Android) / Apple Health (iOS)

  • Step count — displayed as an informational stat on the home screen
  • Heart rate — displayed as an informational stat on the home screen
  • Sleep duration — displayed as an informational stat on the home screen
  • Body weight — used to auto-fill the weight field in the BMI calculator
  • Height — used to auto-fill the height field in the BMI calculator

Important: Health data read from Google Health Connect or Apple Health is used only within the app to display your stats and pre-fill the BMI calculator. This data is never uploaded to our servers, never stored in our database, and never shared with any third party. It exists only on your device during your current session.

Device & usage data

  • Basic device identifiers required by Firebase services
  • App interaction data used to maintain your session

We do not collect location data, financial information, contacts, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To store and display your BMI tracking history
  • To authenticate you securely via Firebase Authentication and Google Sign-In
  • To synchronize your BMI data across devices in real time via Firebase Firestore
  • To improve app functionality through Firebase Remote Config
  • To display health stats (steps, heart rate, sleep) on your home screen dashboard as a convenience utility — read locally from Health Connect or Apple Health, never transmitted
  • To auto-fill the BMI calculator using your most recent weight and height from Health Connect or Apple Health — read locally, never transmitted

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Google Health Connect

On Android devices, LetsCrackIt optionally integrates with Google Health Connect to read health and fitness data. This integration is entirely optional — the app is fully functional without granting Health Connect access.

Our use of Health Connect data is strictly limited to the following:

  • Data is read on-device only at the time you tap "Connect"
  • Data is displayed within the app and is not stored, uploaded, or shared
  • We only request the minimum permissions necessary (Steps, Heart Rate, Sleep, Weight, Height)
  • You can revoke Health Connect permissions at any time from your device settings

Our use of Google Health Connect data complies with the Health Connect Permissions Policy .

5. Apple Health (iOS)

On iOS devices, LetsCrackIt optionally integrates with Apple Health (HealthKit) to read health and fitness data. This integration is entirely optional.

  • Data is read on-device only at the time you tap "Import from Health app"
  • Data is displayed within the app and is not stored, uploaded, or shared
  • You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time from iOS Settings → Privacy → Health

6. Third-Party Services

LetsCrackIt uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Sign-In – policies.google.com/privacy

7. Data Storage & Security

Your account and BMI history data is stored on Google Firebase servers, protected by industry-standard security measures. A minimal copy of your session is stored locally on your device (via encrypted AsyncStorage) to support offline functionality.

Health data read from Google Health Connect or Apple Health is never stored on our servers — it remains on your device only.

While we implement reasonable safeguards, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention
